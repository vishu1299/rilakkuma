  • What is Rilakkuma x SpoGOMI?

  • 01

    What is SpoGOMI?

    This sport was devised in 2008 by Kenichi Mamizuka, president of Social Sports Initiative Inc., with the motto "Picking up trash is a sport!"

  • 02

    rule

    Participants form teams and compete for points based on the amount and type of trash they pick up in a designated area within a time limit

  • 03

    Eligibility

    ・Teams of 5 people or less
    ・For children (recommended age 5 and up) and adults
    *For safety reasons, teams with only members aged 12 or under must be accompanied by a parent or guardian (at least one parent or person aged 18 or over).
